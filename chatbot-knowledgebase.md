## 🎓 Student Internships & Co-Ops FAQ

### Q1: How do I apply for an internship at Forte Innovations?
**Answer:**
We offer two pathways for student internship applications:
1.  **General Internship Application:** Fill out our official [Internship Application Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSehSBEFprcl928aRVymWhfSEk0Wi2UYQDpBCk-X_NCrei-5Cg/viewform?usp=publish-editor).
2.  **Specific Role Screening:** If you are interested in a specific role listed on our [Careers Page](/career), you can book a 30-minute screening session directly via our Calendly scheduler: [Book a Screening Call](https://calendly.com/contact-forteinnovations/30min).
3.  **Email Submission:** Alternatively, email your resume to [contact@forteinnovations.com](mailto:contact@forteinnovations.com) with the subject *"Internship Application - [Position Name]"*.

---

### Q2: What kind of internship positions do you offer?
**Answer:**
We offer internships and Co-Op programs (0 years of experience required) based in New Jersey, US:
*   **AI Automation Intern / Co-Ops:** Work with our engineering teams to build AI-powered automation solutions, intelligent workflows, and custom LLM integrations.
*   **SAP BTP Developer Intern / Co-Ops:** Learn to build, test, and deploy applications on the SAP Business Technology Platform.
*   **SAP Security Analyst Intern / Co-Ops:** Gain hands-on experience in SAP security audit, role design, access controls, and compliance.

---

### Q3: What is the hands-on experience model for interns?
**Answer:**
Unlike typical programs, our interns work directly on live AI, SAP, and custom software projects. You collaborate directly with senior engineers and solutions architects from day one, gaining real-world experience, mentorship, and professional growth.

## 👩‍💻 Women in Tech Initiative FAQ

### Q1: What is the "Women in Tech" initiative at Forte Innovations?
**Answer:**
Forte Innovations champions representation and equal opportunity for women across engineering, AI, and consulting roles. We actively work to close the industry gap by:
*   Providing dedicated mentorship programs.
*   Creating structured leadership pathways for female engineers.
*   Fostering a highly inclusive and collaborative work environment.
